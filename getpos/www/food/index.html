Store Admin

The Store Admin module streamlines store operations by efficiently managing profiles, catalogs, users, and orders. Detailed reports provide insights into sales performance, customer preferences, and inventory levels. Customer ratings are monitored to gauge satisfaction, and promotional codes can drive sales and attract new customers.

Merchant Admin

Merchant admins oversee and manage the entire business, handling profiles, catalogs, users, and orders. Additionally, they can configure POS and Kitchen Display Systems (KDS) for comprehensive management. Detailed analytics provide insights into franchise performance, enabling data-driven decision-making and strategic planning.

KDS & CDS

The Kitchen Display System (KDS) improves kitchen operations with easy-to-read digital order displays that update in real-time, reducing errors and enhancing order preparation times. The Customer Display System (CDS) shows real-time order status and details, keeping customers informed and engaged, enhancing their overall dining experience.

Business Admin

Customizable settings allow them to tailor the system to meet specific client needs, ensuring personalized service. This role focuses on maintaining client relationships, configuring the system for various business requirements, and enhancing overall service delivery, contributing to the long-term success of the business.

Customer Website and Mobile App

The customer-facing features provide a seamless experience with easy login, profile management, product browsing, and secure checkout. Real-time order tracking allows customers to monitor their orders from preparation to delivery. These features ensure a user-friendly and convenient shopping experience.

POS and Table Management (Front-End)

POS and Table Management front-end functionalities enhance daily operations. POS users can log in securely, manage shifts, create and manage orders swiftly, and handle customer details and reservations. Table management features allow staff to view available tables, assign reservations, and update statuses in real-time.
